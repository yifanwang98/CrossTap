Cross Tap
Description
Cross Tap is an interesting and challenging puzzle game. Puzzles can be solved simply by tapping on square grids. Each tap changes the color of the row and column, and your task is to make them all light blue.
Core Features:
• Time limited challenge mode
• Over 50 different regular levels
• Collect stars to unlock backgrounds
• Well-balanced difficulty
